DIY Ideas for Plastic Recylables

Below are five creative ideas to reuse the plastic wastes.

1. Plastic Bottle Planter

Tools Needed:

  • Any plastic bottle
  • Paint
  • Art knife
  • Color markers
  • Plants or Candies

Instructions:

    1. Cut out the shape of the cat or any animals you'd like
    2. Paint the entire bottle that is cutted out
    3. Draw faces on the bottle
    4. The bottle could be used as a planter or a candy holder

2. Piggy Bank

Tools Needed:

  • Any plastic bottle
  • Fabrics
  • Glue and/or tape
  • Color markers or paint spray
  • Four additional bottle caps or other tools that can be used as the pig's legs

Instructions:

    1. Cut out ears and two circle shapes from a fabric
    2. Cut a slot on the bottle
    3. Glue the ears onto the bottle and the two circles onto the bottle cap
    4. Wrap a piece of fabric around the body of the bottle and then tape or glue it
    5. Glue the four bottle caps to the bottom of the bottle as the pig's legs

3.Wall Handle

Tools Needed:

  • Empty detergent bottle
  • Any decorations you'd like
  • Hotglue

Instructions:

    1. Hotglue the detergent bottle to the wall with the handle on the bottom
    2. Make the eyes out of fuzzy balls or other used tools
    3. Glue it on the detergent bottle like the image

4. Bouquet Lamp

Tools Needed:

  • Around 35 used plastic bottles
  • Art knife
  • Glue and/or tape
  • Lightbulb
  • Light

Instructions:

    1. Cut out the end part of the bottles
    2. Glue approximately 7 end parts into a flower
    3. Glue approximately 5 flowers into a sphere
    4. Put in the lightbulb and place it on the stand

5. Spoon Lamp

Tools Needed:

  • Any big plastic bottle
  • Used plastic spoons
  • Hotglue
  • Lightbulb

Instructions:

    1. Cut out the handles on the spoon
    2. Hotglue the spoons in the pattern shown in the picture
    3. Put in the lightbulb and hang the lamp on the ceiling
