DIY Ideas for Paper Recylables

Below are four creative ideas to reuse the plastic wastes.

1. Magazine Envelopes

Tools Needed:

  • Scissor
  • Magazine
  • An envelope
  • Glue
  • Sharpie

Instructions:

    1. Tear off the page that has the design you'd like
    2. Unfold the made envelope
    3. Trace out the flat pattern onto the teared page
    4. Fold it from all four sides as shown in step 6 of the image
    5. Glue the bottom flap down into the two sides
    6. Fold the top flap, but do not glue it until it's ready to use

2. Weave Carton

Tools Needed:

  • Any paper carton or cardboard boxes
  • Plastic bag or newspaper
  • Scissor

Instructions:

    1. Cut the carton into the pattern shown in step 1 of the image
    2. Cut each flap into five ¾" (20 mm) sections
    3. Cut the flatten plastic ot newspaper into long strips that's about 1 inch in width
    4. Flip the carton so that the back is facing up
    5. Weave the plastic bag like step 4 of the image and continue to weave it until it appears like a basket

3. Stationary Caddy

Tools Needed:

  • Used shoe box or empty tissue box
  • Toilet paper roll
  • Glue
  • Pattern papers or construction papers

Instructions:

    1. If it's a tissue box, cut off the entire top
    2. Wrap the pattern paper around the box and glue it
    3. Glue the toliet paper rolls onto the box and try to fill in all the space

    4. Gift Bags

    Tools Needed:

    • Magazine or newspaper
    • Ruler
    • Glue
    • Strings or ribbons
    • Hole punch

    Instructions:

      1. Take out staples (if using magazines) and pick out the pages you want to use
      2. Make a few folds:From the left, 3 inches, 5 inches, 3 inches and 5 inches.
      3. Fold the left over on the right
      4. Adjust the measurements according to your paper size as long as the two smaller folds and two larger folds are the same
      5. Fold the top ¾ in and bottom 2.5 inch
      6. Put glue on the smallest flap (rightmost) and the top flap.
      7. Lay the right flap over the left flap so a tube is formed. Fold the top flap in the bag (this is the opening of the bag)
      8. Fold the bottom like you are wrapping a present and use glue to keep the flaps together
      9. Punch 2 holes in each side of the bag and string some ribbon or whatever through. Secure with a knot at the ends.
