DIY Ideas for Glass Recylables

Below are two creative ideas to reuse glass.

1. Chalkboard Mason Jars

Tools Needed:

  • Clean glass jars
  • Chalk paint
  • Tape
  • Brush
  • Water in a bowl

Instructions:

    1. Use tape to border the area you want to paint on
    2. Paint the area (multiple layers if needed)
    3. When dry, peel tape off and you're done!

2. Mason Jar Lights

Tools Needed:

  • Any glass jars
  • A string of miniature LED ligts
  • Adhesive dots

Instructions:

    1. Clean the mason jar
    2. Attach the adhesive dot to the cover of the can lid as center as possible
    3. Peel off the paper back and press the battery pack of the LED strand onto the adhesive dot
    4. Place the LED lights into the mason jar and close the led
