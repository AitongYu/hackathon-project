Is this recyclable? What can I remake with this?

"Please recycle!" This phrase is said from many voices,

but do people really know what to recycle with these broad categories?

Plastic, for example, is only recyclable when it is clean,

and only certain plastic can be recycled.

If you don't know whether or not the waste is recyclable,

enter the item on the search bar,and you will get an instant answer.
